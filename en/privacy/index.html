Privacy Policy

Last updated · 1 May 2026 · Almobadir

We respect your privacy. This policy explains plainly: what data we collect, why, who we share it with, and how you can control or delete it.

1 · Three-line summary

  • We collect the minimum data necessary to run the site and send the newsletter — nothing else.
  • We don't sell your data. We don't use ad trackers. We don't set advertising cookies.
  • You can unsubscribe or request deletion at any time, in one click.

2 · Who we are (data controller)

"Almobadir" — business and finance media network across the Arab world. For privacy questions: hello@almobadir.com.

3 · What we collect

a · Newsletter signup data

When you subscribe to the newsletter, we collect:

  • Your email address — to send the weekly newsletter, and only that
  • The signup timestamp — for legal compliance (proof of consent)
  • The signup source (which page you signed up from) — for internal site improvements

b · Anonymous analytics

We use Plausible Analytics — a privacy-respecting tool that uses no cookies and collects no personal information. We see aggregate visit counts, popular pages, and referral sources. We do not see who you are.

c · Automatic technical data

When you visit the site, the hosting server (GitHub Pages) records standard logs: IP address, browser type, requested page. These logs are retained by GitHub for 24 hours for security purposes only, then deleted.

5 · Who we share data with

We share the minimum necessary with service providers running the site, only to deliver these services:

  • GitHub Pages — hosting · privacy policy
  • Plausible Analytics — visit analytics · privacy policy
  • Cloudflare (when domain is wired) — content delivery · privacy policy
  • Google Fonts — interface fonts · privacy policy
  • esm.sh / Unsplash — libraries and images · do not receive personal data

When the newsletter formally launches, we'll add the email service provider (Buttondown or its successor) to this list before sending any email.

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it for advertising or commercial purposes.

6 · Your rights (and how to exercise them)

Under Saudi PDPL and EU GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Delete entirely ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to specific processing
  • Withdraw consent any time (unsubscribing = withdrawing consent)
  • Portability to another provider

To exercise any of these, email hello@almobadir.com — we respond within 30 days (usually much faster).

7 · Cookies and local storage

We don't use tracking or advertising cookies. We use browser localStorage to save:

  • Your cookie-consent preference (if you chose "I agree")
  • Your newsletter subscription state (so the form doesn't re-prompt)

This data stays on your device only — it's not sent to us. To clear it: clear browser data for this site.

8 · Retention

  • Newsletter email: while subscribed + 30 days after unsubscribe (to prevent accidental re-add)
  • Server logs: 24 hours (GitHub Pages)
  • Plausible analytics: aggregated without personal identity, retained without expiration

9 · Data security

We use HTTPS site-wide. The service providers listed above protect data using industry-standard practices. We do not store sensitive data (e.g., credit cards, passwords).

10 · Minors

The site is not directed at people under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you discover a minor has submitted data, email us and we'll delete it immediately.

11 · Cross-border transfer

Some data may be processed on servers in the United States or Europe via the providers listed above. All comply with internationally recognized data-protection standards.

12 · Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we'll notify newsletter subscribers by email and update the "last updated" date at the top. Minor changes (rewording, typo fixes) don't require notification.

13 · Contact + complaints

Any privacy question or complaint:

  • Direct email: hello@almobadir.com
  • If we don't resolve your complaint satisfactorily within 30 days, you have the right to file a complaint with the data protection authority in your country (the Saudi Data & AI Authority for Saudi residents, the relevant EU authority for EU residents, etc.).
