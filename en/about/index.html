The story

Almobadir began with a simple observation: the most complex ideas in business and finance can be told with the pull of a thriller film. Serious knowledge has no obligation to be boring. And because the Arab reader is no less sharp than a Bloomberg or Stratechery reader, they deserved a phrasing that lives up to them — not a recycled translation.

Since then, the network has grown into seven channels on Instagram, four on TikTok, and an executional marketing agency serving brands that want to reach the same audience through considered content, not surface advertising.

Editorial philosophy

Content that replaces full courses. That's the promise on the cover of every piece we make. We don't summarize the news — we choose what deserves to be understood deeply, and tell it in a register worthy of the reader. Five stages, applied to every piece of content: choose, simplify, narrate, design, then listen.
