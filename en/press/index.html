Long (paragraph for feature pieces)

Badr Shaker is the founder of Almobadir, the largest creator-led business-and-finance media network in the Arabic-speaking world. Across 11 channels (seven on Instagram, four on TikTok, plus presences on Threads and YouTube), the network reaches 3.3 million followers — making it Tier-1-scale for attention but creator-led for editorial voice.

Badr launched Almobadir in 2022 with a thesis that took root: the most complex ideas in business and finance can be told with the pull of a thriller film. Serious knowledge has no obligation to be boring. The Arab reader is no less sharp than a Bloomberg or Stratechery reader, and they deserve a phrasing worthy of them — not a recycled translation.

Today the network publishes 25 million monthly views, ships a weekly Arabic newsletter on Sunday mornings, and runs Almobadir Media — its in-house marketing agency — for brands that want to reach the same audience through considered content, not surface advertising. The network's voice spans the full pan-Arab spectrum — Maghreb, Egypt, Levant, and the Gulf — with a unified editorial register that translates across all 22 Arab markets.

Story angles

Three angles that journalists / editors / podcast hosts have asked us about most. We can speak to each on the record:

  • The Arab creator economy at scale. How a 3.3M-follower network operates without a newsroom — operational mechanics, content production rate, the relationship between the editorial network and the executional agency.
  • Building a B2B agency on top of a 3.3M consumer audience. The economics of using owned-media reach as a moat for an agency funnel, instead of paying for it via traditional acquisition.
  • Modern SME advice for the Arab founder. What the comments and DMs across 11 handles reveal about what pan-Arab founders actually want to know — and what the gap looks like vs. the Tier-1 Arabic press.
